## 📖 About The Project

**CPUT-Stays** is a centralized, user-friendly web platform built to solve a real challenge faced by students at the Cape Peninsula University of Technology finding and securing off-campus accommodation.

Our goal: reduce administrative burden, increase transparency, and help students secure housing efficiently so they can focus on their academic year.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔍 **Search & Browse** - Explore available student accommodation listings with ease
- 📅 **Book Online** - Submit and manage accommodation bookings directly through the platform
- 🗂️ **Manage Placements** - Track application status and placement details in one dashboard
- 🔒 **Transparent Process** - Clear, step-by-step placement flow for students and administrators
- 📱 **User-Friendly Interface** - Designed with students in mind — simple, intuitive, accessible

## 🎓 Academic Context

This project was developed as part of a **second-year software development module** at CPUT. The brief required students to identify and solve a real problem within the CPUT community. Our team identified the lack of a centralized accommodation platform as a critical pain point for incoming and returning students.
